Why Now

Too much data – women

The amount of pregnancy-related information is ever-increasing, but most of this information is generic and often irrelevant. Self-reassurance through googling sometimes makes matters worse.

pregnant_woman
Too much data – doctors

Knowledge about pregnancy has increased by >1,000% over the last 80 years. Individual clinician has hundreds of cases to consider at any moment in time.

medical_services
child_care
Global paradigm shift in patient-doctor relationship

Developed countries rapidly moving towards “informed patient” concept when the patients are expected to make decisions about their healthcare based on provision of all relevant information and risk assessment.

How it works…

The Caretek platform provides up to date contextual information (tailored specifically to the pregnant woman’s personal characteristics and history) and help for expectant mothers in real time.

How it works…

It collects, stores and analyses individual pregnancy data cross referencing it with the latest clinical guidelines and ensures that the clinician provides the most appropriate and best possible care.
